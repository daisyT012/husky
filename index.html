About Siberian Huskies

How to pronouce Husky?

Husky word origin:

Breed: Siberian Husky

Breed Group: Working

Characteristics: thickly furred double coat, energetic, athletic, triangular ears, distinctive markings, medium sized, independent, and friendly

Huskies originally developed by the Chukchi people of Northeastern Asia to pull sleds over long distances. They need consistent leadership and plenty of exercise every day. They are not usually barkers but rather howl. They are also not good guard dogs because they do not grasp the concept of strangers and might even greet them with enthusiasm.
